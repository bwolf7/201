A litte about myself

Background

My name is Brandon Wolf. I am 25 years old orginally from Temecula California. After highschool, I decided to take my talents to the United States Navy in 2016. I enlisted as an Avionics Electronics Technician (AT) where I got stationed in Virginia Beach Virginia. I spendt 5 years there where I quickly picked up rank and ended my time in service as an E6. I saw one deployement on the USS Harry S Truman where I led a group of 10 Sailors to the successful repair of 422 avionics items. I had a rough time transistioning back to civilian life after deployement. It was at that time, I knew I couldnt do it again and began my transition out.

My experiences since

On my transistion out, I decided to use the skillbridge program to better myself for the civilian life. I attended Microsoft Software and Systems Academy where I learned the fundamentals of Microsoft Azure. The importance of a cloud system was clear. I quickly found a new passion in the world of tech. That was refreshing since I thought that drive had been lost. During the class there were a few fields that caught my interest the most. Azures Blockchain service was the top of the list. The ability to have an encrypted peer-to-peer service offers so many possibilities. However, my limited coding background quickly halted my learning drive. I knew if I wanted to pursue this field, I would need to be able to code. I used my military benefits to attend CodeFellows coding bootcamp.

Fun Facts

Outside of sitting at my computer, I enjoy going to baseball games. As a SoCal resident, I fell in love with San Diego. The city is the perfect blend of city and beach. I have been going to Padres games since 2013. In the prime of my fandom, I would attend multiple games a week. I have a Padres hat from their 2016 All Star game that I would wear everyday for the last 5 years. Ive seen them at their lowest and have gotten the chance to see them now when they are finally a competitive team. I also like to spend time with family and friends. My sister is my bestfriend and together we go to several concerts in the SoCal area.
